At Colorado Boat Center, service is what keeps you on the water.

From routine maintenance to repairs and upgrades, our team works on a wide range of boats and engines across multiple manufacturers and model years.

Our certified technicians are trained to service and perform warranty work on Barletta, Regal, and Yamaha boats, along with Mercury, MerCruiser, Volvo Penta, Yamaha Outboard, Yamaha Jet, and Indmar, and engines.

24/7 Advantage Program

The 24/7 Advantage Program at Colorado Boat Center is a convenience program designed for CBC boat buyers that allows members to drop off and pick up their boats for service outside normal business hours. With flexible access to the service facility, the program makes it easier to fit maintenance and repairs into your schedule, so you can spend less time planning around service hours and more time enjoying the water.

Drop Off & Pick Up Anytime

Drop off your boat for service any time of day, any day of the week - even after a long day on the water - and pick it up whenever it’s convenient. Simply pay with a credit card and your serviced boat will be ready when you are.

Off-Season Storage

We offer storage starting at $959 for a seven-month term, making the off-season stress-free. Schedule it with your winter service and we’ll take care of the rest, including secure off-site storage and all the coordination behind the scenes. When spring rolls around, we’ll schedule your summarization and have your boat ready for pickup.

Shrinkwrap

Protect your boat from Colorado’s harsh off-season elements with professional shrink wrap, starting at $444. This tight, weather resistant seal helps guard against snow, moisture, dirt, and UV exposure throughout the winter months. It’s a simple step that goes a long way in preserving your boat’s condition and getting you ready for a smooth start come spring.
