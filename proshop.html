Since 2004, Colorado Boat Center has hosted Women Making Waves, a hands-on boating seminar designed to help women build confidence and skills on the water. Its popularity led to the creation of Men in the Know, a similar program for men. Both events provide a relaxed, supportive learning environment where participants can practice essential boating skills—including trailering, launching, docking, knot tying, and water safety—guided by the knowledgeable CBC team.

Pro Shop FAQs

Q. What products can I find in the CBC Pro Shop?

A. Our Pro Shop carries a wide selection of boating gear, watersports equipment, safety items, accessories, and essentials to help you get the most out of every day on the water.

Q. Do you carry watersports gear?

A. Yes! We stock equipment for popular watersports including wakeboarding, tubing, and other towable activities.

Q. Can your staff help me choose the right gear?

A. Absolutely. Our knowledgeable team members are experienced boaters who can recommend the best products based on your boat, activities, and experience level.

Q. Do you carry safety equipment?

A. Yes, we offer essential safety gear such as life jackets, throwable devices, and other boating safety products.

Q. Can I purchase replacement accessories for my boat?

A. Yes. Our Pro Shop carries a variety of replacement accessories and boating essentials to keep your boat fully equipped.

Q. Do you offer gear for beginners?

A. Definitely. Our team can help first-time boaters select the right gear and equipment to start enjoying the water safely and confidently.

Q. Can I ask questions about boating equipment before buying?

A. Of course. Our staff is always happy to answer questions and help you understand which gear will work best for your needs.

Q. Where is the Pro Shop located?

A. The Pro Shop is conveniently located at Colorado Boat Center, making it easy to stop in while shopping for boats, scheduling service, or preparing for your next outing.
